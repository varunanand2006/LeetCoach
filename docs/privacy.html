LeetCoach Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

LeetCoach ("the extension") is an AI coding coach sidebar for LeetCode. This policy describes what data is collected and how it is used.

What we collect

When you use LeetCoach on a LeetCode problem page, the extension reads your LeetCode username from the page and sends it to our servers solely to track your weekly usage against the free tier limit (100 requests per week). Your username, request count, and the dates of your first and most recent use are stored in a database hosted on AWS (us-east-1).

We also read your current code, the problem description, and your chat messages in order to generate AI responses. This content is transmitted to our server to call the AI model but is never stored — it is processed in memory and discarded.

What we do not collect

We do not collect passwords, email addresses, or any information beyond what is described above. We do not use cookies, analytics services, or advertising trackers.

How we use your data

Your username and usage counts are used only to enforce the weekly request limit. We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to any third party.

Data retention and deletion

Usage records are retained until you request deletion. To request deletion of your data, contact us at varun.anand2006@gmail.com with your LeetCode username.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, the "Last updated" date above will be updated.
